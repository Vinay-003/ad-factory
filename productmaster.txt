1. What is Obesity Killer Kit?
Obesity Killer Kit is a doctor-formulated Ayurvedic weight-loss system designed for
overweight and obese adults who want to lose weight in a safe, natural, and practical way. It
is built for people who want fast visible results without relying on injections, crash diets,
hard-to-sustain gym routines, or extreme lifestyle changes that are difficult to maintain.
Rather than being a standalone supplement, it is designed as a structured, guided system
that makes weight loss easier to follow in real life.
At its core, Obesity Killer Kit offers a simple promise: lose 3 to 5 kg in 15 days through a
natural, easy-to-follow process that does not leave the user feeling hungry, weak, or
overwhelmed. The product is built around five core ideas: fast results, an Ayurvedic and
all-natural formulation, no side effects, ease of following, and long-term sustainability.
2. What problem does it solve?
The product is built for a common failure pattern in weight loss. Many people do not fail
because they lack intent. They fail because most methods create too much friction. Strict
diets create hunger. Meal replacements feel unnatural. Intense workout plans are hard to
maintain. Injections are scary. Medication creates dependency. Stimulant-style products can
feel unsustainable. The result is a cycle of effort, inconsistency, disappointment, and quitting.
Also,
Weight gain is also not always driven only by eating habits or lifestyle choices. For many
people, it is influenced by factors outside their direct control, such as genetics, hormonal
imbalance, medications, and chronic health conditions. These situations often make weight
loss slower, more frustrating, and harder to sustain with standard advice alone.
Obesity Killer Kit is built to solve these friction points. It is designed for people who want to
lose weight without feeling hungry, weak, confused, or forced into an unrealistic routine. It is
also relevant for people whose weight-loss journey is made harder by underlying biological
or medical factors, because it aims to make the process easier to follow, more manageable,
and more sustainable in real life.

6. What comes in the kit?
Obesity Killer Kit is a physical boxed course built around three main formulation components
plus support material and tracking tools. The live product page also sells it in 15-day, 30-day,
and 2 x 30-day partner variants, while your internal usage language refers to 15-day, 30-day,
and 60-day buying options. The standardized pricing you confirmed is ₹5,800 for 15 days,
₹10,000 for 30 days, and ₹17,000 for 60 days.
OK Liquid
OK Liquid is the core part of the system. It comes as 15 pre-measured pouches in a 15-day
kit, one for each day. Its role is to reduce hunger, increase satiety, help the user eat less
without feeling weak, and support the body with nutrition during the process. You confirmed
that the OK Liquid provides 350 to 400 kcal of nutrition. It is taken every morning on an
empty stomach after warming the pouch in hot water. No solid food should be consumed for
the next 4 hours, though zero-calorie drinks such as tea, coffee, lemon water, lemon tea, and
jeera water can be taken.
OK Tablet
OK Tablet is taken at night, 15 minutes before bed. It is positioned as digestive support that
helps with bloating, acidity, and gas. It can also continue to be used after the main course as
part of gut health support.
OK Powder
OK Powder is taken at night, 15 minutes before bed, followed by warm water. It supports
digestion, nutrient absorption, and waste excretion. Like the tablet, it can continue to play a
role beyond the main course.
Dried Amla
Dried amla is taken after the liquid. It acts as a natural mouth freshener, helps with taste
balance, and supports digestion.
Daily Tracker
The tracker is a core part of the system, not just an accessory. Users record daily weight,
meals, steps, water, and sleep, then share it with the team on WhatsApp. This improves
accountability, helps the support team guide the user using real data, and is part of refund
eligibility.
Guide and support
The kit includes an information booklet, a list of allowed and not allowed foods, and a 7-day
suggested diet plan for users who want more step-by-step direction. Users can also
choose from allowed foods according to convenience. Dedicated chat support is available to
answer questions, keep users on track, and nudge tracker sharing.

8. How does it work?
Obesity Killer Kit is designed as a multi-action Ayurvedic weight-loss system, not a
single-function product. Its three components work together to support weight loss through
complementary mechanisms rather than relying on just one lever.
The OK Liquid primarily helps on the intake side. It reduces hunger and cravings, improves
satiety, and makes it easier for the user to maintain a caloric deficit without feeling weak or
depleted. This is important because most weight-loss plans fail not in theory, but in
day-to-day adherence. By making lower intake easier to sustain, the OK Liquid helps turn
caloric deficit from a struggle into a more manageable routine.
The OK Tablet and OK Powder work on the body’s processing side. They are designed to
support digestion, gut comfort, and metabolic function during the course. By helping reduce
issues such as bloating, acidity, gas, constipation, and heaviness, they improve digestive
efficiency and support a lighter, more comfortable physiological state. This matters because
better digestive function often improves compliance, energy, and overall ease of following
the program.
Taken together, the system works on both sides of the equation. The OK Liquid helps reduce
calorie intake. The OK Tablet and OK Powder support the body’s ability to digest,
metabolize, and eliminate more effectively. So the kit is not positioned merely as an
appetite-control product, but as a more holistic weight-loss system that supports both lower
intake and better internal functioning.
From an Ayurvedic perspective, the kit is designed to support pachakagni and dhatvagni,
reduce the accumulation of aam, and promote detoxification. In practical terms, this means
the course is intended not only to support fat loss, but also to help the body feel lighter,
cleaner, and more balanced over time. This is why Obesity Killer Kit is positioned as more
than a short-term weight-loss intervention. It is designed as a structured Ayurvedic system
that supports fast visible results while remaining natural, practical, and sustainable.
