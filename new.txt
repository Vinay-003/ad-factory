Single Source of Truth: Product Foundation Document

1. What is Obesity Killer Kit?
Obesity Killer Kit is a doctor-formulated Ayurvedic weight-loss system designed for overweight and obese adults who want to lose weight in a safe, natural, and practical way.
It is built for people who want fast visible results without relying on injections, crash diets, hard-to-sustain gym routines, or extreme lifestyle changes.
It is not positioned as a standalone supplement. It is a structured, guided system built for real-life adherence.
Core promise: lose 3 to 5 kg in 15 days through a natural, easy-to-follow process that does not leave the user feeling hungry, weak, or overwhelmed.

2. What problem does it solve?
Most users do not fail from lack of intent. They fail because methods are hard to sustain: strict diets create hunger, intense routines create drop-off, and high-friction methods create inconsistency.
For many people, weight gain is also influenced by genetics, hormonal imbalance, medications, and chronic conditions.
Obesity Killer Kit is designed to reduce these friction points and make adherence easier in day-to-day life.

3. What is the customer hiring the product to do?
Surface job: lose weight.
Deeper job: lose weight in a way that feels fast, safe, believable, and practical to continue.
Functional job: reduce hunger and cravings, support caloric-deficit consistency, remove guesswork through a guided routine.
Emotional job: restore belief, control, and consistency after repeated failure.

4. Who is it for?
Adults with BMI above 25 who want natural, guided, easy-to-follow weight-loss support.
Common fit profiles: stubborn fat, belly fat, cravings, emotional eating, low energy, slow metabolism, repeated diet failure.
Also relevant for users dealing with obesity-linked concerns such as PCOD, PCOS, thyroid imbalance, high blood pressure, fatty liver, diabetes, and IBS (not a cure claim).
Primary demand fit: users wanting fast visible results without injections or hardcore workout dependency.

5. Who is it not for?
Do not use for:
- pregnant women
- women within 6 months postpartum
- people actively taking insulin

Doctor-review caution before use:
- people on multiple psychiatric medications
- people with multiple surgery history

Do not position for users below 18 years of age.

Physical boxed system with three formulation components plus support tools.

8. How does it work?
The system works through complementary actions:
- Intake side: OK Liquid helps reduce hunger and cravings and supports satiety, making lower intake easier.
- Processing side: OK Tablet and OK Powder support digestion and gut comfort, helping consistency and comfort.

Combined mechanism: easier adherence to lower intake plus better digestive comfort, resulting in practical and sustainable weight-loss support.

Ayurvedic framing: supports pachakagni and dhatvagni, reduces aam accumulation, and supports detoxification.
