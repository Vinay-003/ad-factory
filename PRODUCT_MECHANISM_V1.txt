PRODUCT_MECHANISM_V1 - OBESITY KILLER KIT (ALIGNED)

SECTION 0: CORE POSITIONING
This product is not positioned as a standalone fat-burner.
This is a structured obesity and weight-loss support protocol that improves adherence through appetite and digestion support.

SECTION 1: COMPONENT ROLES
1) OK Liquid (morning)
- Taken first thing on waking, empty stomach.
- Supports fullness and reduces cravings.
- Helps shift from clock-based eating to true-hunger eating.
- Supports lower unnecessary intake without hunger-led weakness.

2) OK Tablet (night)
- Taken around 15 minutes before sleep.
- Supports digestive comfort (gas, bloating, acidity support).

3) OK Powder (night)
- Taken with tablet, around 15 minutes before sleep, followed by warm water.
- Supports digestion, absorption, and elimination comfort.

SECTION 2: DAILY EXECUTION PROTOCOL
- Morning: OK Liquid on empty stomach.
- Post-liquid: 4-hour no-solid-food window (zero-calorie drinks allowed).
- Eating behavior: eat only on true hunger; no fixed meal timing.
- Night: OK Tablet + 1/2 tsp OK Powder before sleep.

SECTION 3: DIET AND LIFESTYLE ENVELOPE
Allowed food base:
- vegetables, salads, soups, fruits
- up to 30g complex carbs (millets/oats) only if still hungry

Restricted foods:
- non-vegetarian food
- alcohol
- dairy except milk in tea/coffee
- wheat chapati, rice, dal
- nuts

Lifestyle support:
- around 4 liters water/day
- 5000 to 7000 steps/day or pranayam

SECTION 4: BEHAVIORAL TRANSFORMATION MODEL
Old pattern:
- eating by clock, cues, stress, or availability

New pattern with protocol:
- eating by true hunger
- less impulsive snacking
- smaller practical portions
- steadier adherence through lower friction

SECTION 5: FUNCTIONAL OUTCOME MODEL
Mechanism chain:
1) appetite and craving pressure reduce
2) unnecessary intake drops
3) digestion comfort improves daily compliance
4) caloric-deficit adherence becomes easier
5) weight-loss progression becomes more achievable in real life

SECTION 6: CLAIM BOUNDARIES
Allowed:
- helps reduce hunger and cravings
- helps users stay in a caloric deficit more consistently
- supports digestion and gut comfort
- supports practical obesity and weight-loss efforts

Not allowed:
- burns fat rapidly by itself
- melts fat without effort
- works even with unlimited junk food
- overrides all discipline

SECTION 7: ON-IMAGE COPY APPLICATION RULE
Every headline/subheadline that uses mechanism language must also name the goal directly.
Approved framing examples:
- appetite control for weight loss
- digestion support for easier weight-loss adherence
- obesity-support routine for fast visible weight-loss progress

Avoid abstract-only mechanism lines such as:
- control your routine
- reset your day
- transform consistency
unless the same unit also clearly states weight loss or obesity context.

SECTION 8: OPERATING SUMMARY
Obesity Killer Kit works as a guided protocol that combines appetite support, eating-behavior correction, and digestion support so users can follow a realistic weight-loss process more consistently.
