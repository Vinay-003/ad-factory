SECTION 1: PRODUCT COMPONENTS
------------------------------------------------------------

1. OK Liquid (Morning)
- Timing: Empty stomach (start of day)
- Practical timing rule: Take first thing upon waking, even if wake-up time is unusual
- Follow-up rule: 4-hour fasting window after taking it; tea/coffee may be allowed within protocol guidance from `faq.txt`
- Role: Appetite stabilization + craving reduction

2. OK Powder (Night)
- Timing: Before sleep
- Practical timing rule: about 15 minutes before bedtime
- Role: Digestion support + reduce heaviness

3. OK Tablet (Night)
- Timing: With powder
- Practical timing rule: keep about a 2-hour gap between dinner and bedtime when following the full protocol
- Role: Metabolic support (mild) + digestion assistance

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2: FUNCTIONAL EFFECTS (REALISTIC)
------------------------------------------------------------

OK Liquid:
- Creates prolonged fullness sensation
- Digests slowly and can stay filling for much of the day (often described as a 7-8 hour effect in `faq.txt`)
- Reduces urge to eat early in the day
- Dampens habitual eating triggers
- Reduces frequency of meals/snacking
- Can help people move from clock-based eating toward true-hunger eating
- Is positioned as providing support for satiety without a crash-diet feel

OK Powder:
- Helps reduce bloating and heaviness
- Improves digestion after irregular meals
- Reduces discomfort from late eating
- Can help reduce constipation-related discomfort

OK Tablet:
- Supports digestion processes
- Supports metabolic efficiency (non-aggressive)
- Complements powder effects
- Can help reduce gas, bloating, and acidity when used with powder

SYSTEM EFFECT:
- Lower eating frequency
- Lower portion size
- Reduced impulsive intake
- More stable daily energy
- Easier adherence because the system is simpler than strict diet/gym plans

------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3: ROOT PROBLEM THIS PRODUCT TARGETS
------------------------------------------------------------

This product is designed for people whose weight gain is caused by:

- Random eating (not structured overeating)
- Frequent snacking
- Eating out of boredom/stress
- Irregular meal timing
- Late-night eating
- Poor digestion leading to heaviness and inconsistency

NOT primarily for:
- Athletes optimizing body fat %
- Highly disciplined diet followers
